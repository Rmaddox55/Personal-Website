Pi Lambda Phi Fraternity

In the Spring Semester of 2020, I joined the re-founding father class of Pi Lambda Phi Fraternity at the University of Cincinnati. I currently am the Web Administrator for the chapter website and the Vice President of Programming. Throughout my involvement with Pi Lambda Phi, I have gained valuable leadership skills being in charge of the programming comittee. I have also gained valuable teamwork skills through coordinating with my comittee to oversee events, as well as coordinating with others on the executive board to oversee general chapter operations.

Association for Computing Machinery at UC

I joined ACM@UC the Fall of my Sophomore year. I was looking for a way to boost my resume since I had no previous technical job experience when searching for my first Co-Op, and this club helped me do just that. As a member, I attend bi-weekly meetings that go-over various technical topics. These topics include technical interviews, could computing, version control, UI/UX design, full stack development, project design frameworks, etc.
